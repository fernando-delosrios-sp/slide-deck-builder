## Overview

Softens the technical edge into a sterile but friendly rounded 'clay' 3D style suitable for medical and biotech presentations.

## Visual Execution

**Motif:** DNA helixes, fluid network nodes, secure molecular structures, interlocking medical pill shapes.

**Execution:** Claymorphism (soft rounded 3D UI), smooth drop shadows, clean white layouts, avoiding sharp/aggressive angles.

**Mode:** light
